The Outbreak
One day, Quahog remained peaceful. It was doing business as usual all the way until Boyfriend went into the show, which lured the darkness to attack the city. When people realized what it was, they did not even care until one person got corrupted. Boyfriend was looking for the Griffin's house to warn them, but due to Boyfriend's approach, Quahog suffered a deluge of madness, where citizens got sucked in one by one. Channel 5 soon reports that The Darkness is so bad, that not even the mayor's action could contain it.

Meanwhile, the Griffins were riding in their car[1] trying to leave Quahog when Bruce and Jeffrey, also attempting to escape in their car, crash into the Darkness and get corrupted. This prompts Peter and Lois to exit the car. While Peter's guard was down, Bruce comes up from behind and slits his neck. As the corruption overtakes his body, Lois starts to scream out Peter's name, which causes her to let her guard down and allow Jeffrey to spit corruption onto the right side of her face. Meg reacts to the corruption of Lois, but then gets pulled through the window and gets chopped up by Bruce or Jeffrey. Chris starts to cry about his family’s supposed death, with Peter soon looming behind him and presumably making him suffer the same fate. After the corruption of Chris, the rest of the family fuses into Peter and he teleports from the edge of Quahog to the front of his house. After he laughs, he opens the door and peers into the house, presumably spotting Boyfriend in his living room, thus beginning A Family Guy.

A Family Guy
Boyfriend, who has lots of bruises and slightly torn clothes enters the house to see what's going on. He sees a shadow and hears a corrupted voice. Moments later, Peter reveals himself. Then, Stewie, who has messy hair, and Brian, who has some duct tape on his nose, enter the room as Peter roars. Then Meg and Chris emerge from Peter's left and right side as Lois creepily gets out of Peter as she screams "Someone needs a Diaper CHANGE!". A minute or two later, Lois throws Boyfriend out of a window as she jumps out of Peter's body. A minute later, Lois roars as Peter repeats "ONE OF US!" several times.

After that, Stewie and Brian run upstairs to Stewie's room, but the darkness caught Brian, as he tries to escape.
Rotten Family
Stewie in his room gets his favourite teddy bear, Rupert, as Brian, who is borderline corrupted with glowing eyes, walks slowly in Stewie's room. Then Peter enters Stewie's room as well. Seconds later, Brian officially fully corrupts as he whispers "GRRRRR....". He and Peter reveal themselves as the darkness floods Stewie's room. Moments Later, Peter starts to uncorrupt as Lois enters the room. Eventually, Peter breaks free and has to fight against Brian and Lois. A couple of minutes later, Stewie teleports to escape, but Lois slashes him as he successfully teleports.

Peter, now being alone, jumps out the window so he doesn't get corrupted. Boyfriend saves him as they both dash out of the city, starting Death Lives.
Fashioned Values
Quagmire and Joe are shown facing off against Corrupted Cleveland. Cleveland fuses with Peter's evil twin, Retep, to throw them both off track. Quagmire shoots them both several times, saving Cleveland. What happened to Retep is unknown. The guys separate after facing Retep, with Quagmire walked to the heart of Quahog, “supposedly” meeting Stewie and starting Twinkle.
Death Lives
Peter and BF are on an attempt to escape The Darkness as it chases after them, which causes many civilians to become corrupted and attack other survivors. Eventually, Peter's family, except Stewie and Brian, reveal themselves, causing Peter to regret his decisions, until corrupted Death comes in and tries to stop them. Eventually, Peter and BF successfully escape, but part ways.
Twinkle
While Stewie was successful at escaping the Griffin household, he was unsuccessful at making it out unscathed. He was slashed by Lois mere seconds before he teleported away, causing his corruption. Shortly after Fashioned Values, Quagmire, Joe, and Cleveland are forced out of the clam, and they are forced to split up. Quagmire, as he's walking hears Stewie crying out for help, begging to be saved before becoming fully corrupted. Stewie then mutates into a giant spider corrupted monster and battles Quagmire. Half way through, Stewie stabs Quagmire through the chest, which nearly corrupts Glenn but instead corrupts his entire right hand altogether. However, Quagmire frees himself due to him having every STD in the world, giving him slight immunity to The Darkness. As Stewie returns to normal, he begins breaking free. Quagmire is trying hard in freeing Stewie. In the end, Stewie fully breaks free from the darkness but he, like Quagmire, is severely injured. Stewie then teleports away to a safe universe to reside in.
Joe and Cleveland do not show up
Final Fight
After escaping The Darkness in Death Lives, Peter heads to the airport to escape Quahog. However, corrupt Ernie appears to stop him. He semi-corrupts Peter in this song, but then Quagmire arrives and intervenes to help a Semi corrupted Peter with Ernie and managing to defeat him as he disappears into the shadows. Peter rids the corruption of his body as he and his friends board the plane.
Airborne
The Guys have successfully escaped Quahog's chaos. However, their plan didn't go as expected. Cleveland's step-son, Rallo, was able to sneak on the plane, but unfortunately, he was corrupted. Rallo is shown to be extremely powerful as he corrupts Quagmire half way through the song. Corrupted Quagmire emerges at the end and causes the plane to crash, leaving the guys fates unknown.
Gameplay Mechanics
Mechanic	Description	Example(s)
Health Drain	When it is opponent's turn to sing, for every note that is hit, your health will be drained. Every opponent in the mod uses this mechanic, but there is a difference between most of the opponents.	Only Rallo can quickly consume the player's health. Most opponents rely on spam notes to boost their health.
Near the end of Twinkle, Stewie doesn't drain the player's health. In some songs, the opponents can push the player's health down to one (ex. A Family Guy, first half). In others, it only goes as far as the player's danger zone (ex. Twinkle). In Death Lives, the player's health is drained automatically and it will kill you. To counter this, the player needs to press the spacebar for a rapid boost in health.
Colored notes	Every character has their own notes, depicted by shirt, pants, characteristics, or hair/fur in survivors, but all glitchy and black (and shaking for cosmetic effect) for the opponents, with the sole exception of Homer.	Boyfriend attains his usual notes, but the rest have notes depicted on icon and color of their shirt or pants or hair/fur. For example, Peter has dark green note colors.
Ghost Notes/Fake Notes	When more than one character is singing at the same time, the player only have to hit the arrows of the main character (indicated by the main character's notes being completely visible while the others are more transparent). The rest of the notes will be hit automatically, though, the player can still hit them for extra score and health.	In A Family Guy, the player only need to hit Boyfriend's notes up until he gets thrown out of the fight, and then you only have to Stewie's Notes as Brian's notes will be hit automatically throughout the whole song. In Rotten Family, when Peter breaks free, you don't have to hit his notes up until the end section. In Fashioned Values, the player only needs to hit Quagmire's notes, as Joe's and Cleveland's notes will be hit automatically. In Final Fight, when Quagmire appears, the player doesn't need to hit his notes as it will be hit automatically.
Attacking	In some parts of A Family Guy, an 'ATTACK' popup will appear. Hitting space will make Stewie attack, giving you a small amount of health. This has a cooldown, meaning you cannot spam it. While this mechanic is active, the opponent's health drain CAN kill the player, so the player has to time the attacks to not die.	-
Bullet Arrow Notes	In Fashioned Values, Quagmire will shoot at corrupted Cleveland and Retep as the player hits these notes. At least 10 bullet notes must be collected, and anything below that will result in a failure to save Cleveland, causing a jumpscare to trigger at the final part of the song, thus, causing an early death. For now it's unknown if the large red eye is the Game Over screen or not.	-
Laser Notes	In Twinkle, corrupted Stewie will sometimes shoot lasers at you. The player must hit these notes to dodge them. If not, the player can actually DIE.	-
Punch Notes	In Final Fight, these notes will appear. Hitting them will cause Peter to counter Ernie's attacks by punching. Miss a single note and Peter will get knocked out, thus, corrupting him.	-
False Time	When Quagmire is corrupted during the last 42 seconds of Airborne, the time at the time bar will increase rapidly forever, even after the song ends.	-
